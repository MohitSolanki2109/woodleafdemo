About WoodLeaf Interior

15+ years of delivering premium plywood and interior materials to Rajkot and across Gujarat.

Our Story

Built on Wood, Grounded in Trust

WoodLeaf Interior was founded in 2009 by Mr. Bhavesh Mehta, a passionate carpenter who believed that quality raw materials were the foundation of beautiful furniture. Starting from a small shop in Rajkot, we have grown into one of Gujarat's most respected plywood and interior materials suppliers.

Today, we serve over 500 active clients — including carpenters, interior designers, building contractors, furniture manufacturers, and homeowners across Rajkot, Morbi, Jamnagar, and beyond. Our commitment: right material, right price, right time.

We stock only ISI-certified and factory-direct plywood from India's leading brands including Greenply, Century Ply, Kitply, and Merino.

Our Journey

How We Grew

2009
WoodLeaf Founded
Mr. Bhavesh Mehta opens a small plywood shop in Rajkot with a vision to supply quality materials at fair prices.
2013
Expanded to Laminates & Veneers
Added decorative laminates and veneer sheets to meet growing demand from interior designers.
2017
New Showroom Inaugurated
Moved to a larger 5,000 sq ft showroom with display sections for all product categories.
2020
500+ Client Milestone
Reached over 500 active clients including major contractors, furniture manufacturers, and retail customers.
2024
Digital Presence & Expansion
Launched online catalog and expanded delivery to cover all major cities in the Saurashtra region.
