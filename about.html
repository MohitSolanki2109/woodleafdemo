About WoodLeaf Interiors

Rajkot's trusted supplier of plywood, laminates, veneers & hardware — serving since 2019.

Our Story

Built on Wood, Grounded in Trust

Woodleaf Interiorwas founded with a simple belief — quality raw materials are the foundation of beautiful furniture and lasting construction. Starting from a small showroom on Tagore Road, Rajkot, we have grown into one of the region's most trusted suppliers of plywood, laminates, veneers, and hardware.

Today we serve carpenters, interior designers, building contractors, furniture manufacturers, and homeowners across Rajkot, Morbi, Jamnagar, and beyond. Our promise is simple: right material, right price, right time.

Every product we stock is verified for quality. We carry ISI-certified plywood, high-pressure laminates in hundreds of designs, natural wood veneers, and a full range of furniture hardware — all under one roof.

Our Journey

How We Grew

2019
Woodleaf InteriorFounded
Opened our showroom on Tagore Road, Rajkot — opposite Atul Motors — with a focus on quality plywood and hardware.
2020
Expanded Product Range
Added decorative laminates and veneer sheets to meet growing demand from interior designers and furniture makers.
